Welcome to Nemesia Academy

“Nemesia Academy is an ICSE School by the Congregation of Sisters of Charity of St.Jeanne Antide Thouret. Since 2014, our congregation has been educating the children in Bangalore. We acknowledge that, this is our first challenging venture in Bangalore to address a good value based moral and disciplined education to the children of our Society. Responding to the need of caring the integral formation of children, with the motto “To educate with Love”,
Our school is not just a place of academics; it is a second home for our students. We emphasize the importance of instilling strong values, compassion, and resilience in our young minds. Through a well-rounded education that encompasses academic excellence, arts, sports, and community engagement, we strive to nurture compassionate and well-balanced individuals. Safety is our utmost priority. We maintain a secure and supportive atmosphere where our students can explore, experiment, and grow without inhibition. We encourage open communication between parents, teachers, and students to ensure that every child receives the guidance and care they need.
